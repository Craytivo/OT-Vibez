The day of, I got a phone call and text from OT Vibez and the owner himself talked me through what to expect during our booking. Oshane (I hope I spelled that right) is personable and easy to talk to, and answered all the questions I could possibly have.

The rehearsal space is spacious so we could spread out comfortably. Even at 6 people, we still had lots of room. The drum kit is on the far end with extra sound proofing, which my ears appreciated. It's clean and tidy, there's chairs, and the LED lighting earns OT Vibez it's name 👌
