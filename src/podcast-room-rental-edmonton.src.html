Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a podcast recording studio in Edmonton without a videographer?

Yes. You can book our room-only video and podcast setup and bring your own production team.

What is included in full video podcast production?

Full production includes videographer Tomiwa Ladejoh, Sony FX30 + Sony ZV-E10 camera setup, pro microphones, wireless mics, and studio lighting with multiple background options.

What are your booking and editing rules?

Sessions are booked in 1-hour blocks. Editing is available as an add-on at CA$50/hour, measured 1:1 with usable content.

How much does podcast video production cost?

Pricing starts at CA$195 for 1 hour and scales to CA$1,560 for 8 hours, with transparent hourly increments.
